About Nico Bleh

With over 15 years of leadership experience in high-impact tech roles, Nico Bleh has guided startups and scaleups to achieve ambitious technical and business objectives—from defining strategic technology roadmaps and architecting scalable SaaS platforms to leading agile transformations and integrating generative AI and chatbot solutions. His collaborative approach blends strategic vision with hands-on execution, enabling teams to streamline processes, innovate rapidly, and drive sustainable growth.

Professional Journey

  • 2024–2025: Chief Technology Officer at Opteinics – Defined and executed strategic technology roadmaps driving platform growth.
  • 2023-2024: Head of Technology at Textbroker – Led infrastructure modernization and product architecture redesign.
  • 2022–2023: Department Head Web at onOffice – Managed a team of 22 delivering unified web solutions.
  • 2020–2021: CTO at Branchbob – Oversaw merchant dashboard development and SaaS roadmap delivery.
  • 2016–2020: CTO/Head of IT at Creditshelf – Scaled technical teams and led technology through IPO.

My Approach

I partner closely with leadership to understand both business objectives and technical challenges. My collaborative process involves rapid discovery, iterative development, and transparent communication to ensure alignment and deliver measurable outcomes.

Whether you need to scale your engineering team, optimize existing systems, or embark on a new product initiative, my goal is to provide pragmatic solutions that drive efficiency, innovation, and sustainable growth.
